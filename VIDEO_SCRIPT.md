- "First, you enter your details in natural language - no forms, no templates to fill"

- Click "Generate ATS-Perfect Resume"
- "The AI processes your prompt and creates a structured resume with:"

## Recording Tips
1. Use screen recording software (OBS, Loom, or Windows Game Bar)
2. Record in 1080p resolution
3. Show both the UI and the results
4. Speak clearly and at moderate pace
5. Keep total video under 5 minutes
6. Include cursor highlighting for important clicks
7. Test audio levels before recording
8. Upload to your GitHub repository
